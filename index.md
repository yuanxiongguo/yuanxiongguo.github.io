**About:** I am currently an Assistant Professor in the [Department of Information Systems and Cyber Security](https://business.utsa.edu/information-systems-cyber-security/) at [The University of Texas at San Antonio](https://www.utsa.edu/). I also hold a courtesy apppointment in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Florida in July 2014. 

My current research focuses on big data analytics, distributed machine learning, security and privacy with applications to Internet of things and edge computing.
